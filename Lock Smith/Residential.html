Service & Repairs

Regular servicing of your locks will extend their life and improve their reliability. As a guide, we recommend servicing your locks every 5 years or so to ensure smooth operation and peace of mind for you and your family Existing locks may also need to be repaired from time to time which could be much less expensive than installing brand new locks. Our technician will inspect your locks and let you know if this is a viable alternative for you.

Rekeying

Rekeying your existing locks saves the cost of replacing old locks with new locks. If you have lost a set of keys or perhaps had them stolen then this may be the most cost-effective solution for you. In most cases, we can simply replace the barrel and supply you with new keys.

Keying Alike

This is ideal for many of our customers as it means that one key can operate all the locks in your home. No need to carry a bunch of keys around anymore. Very convenient and very cost-effective.
